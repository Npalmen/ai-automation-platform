# Product Roadmap

## Vision

Build an invisible AI layer that automates workflows across industries.

---

## Stage 1 – MVP (Weeks 1–2)

Goal: First paying customer

Features:
- Lead automation
- Approval system
- Action execution
- Minimal UI

Target user:
- Small business (electrician, contractor)

---

## Stage 2 – Early Product (Weeks 3–6)

Goal: Repeatable sales

Features:
- Real integrations
- Dashboard
- Manual review workflows
- Multi-tenant config

---

## Stage 3 – Scale (Months 2–4)

Goal: System stability

Features:
- Async processing
- Retry systems
- Monitoring
- Logging

---

## Stage 4 – Platform (Months 4–8)

Goal: General automation engine

Features:
- Workflow builder
- Configurable pipelines
- Industry templates

---

## Stage 5 – Expansion

Goal: Market dominance

Features:
- Document automation (folder scanner)
- AI decision tuning
- Marketplace of workflows

---

## Business Model

- Subscription per company
- Usage per job
- Premium features (advanced workflows)

---
